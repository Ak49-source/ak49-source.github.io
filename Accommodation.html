Accommodations

Himachal brings to you a wide range of accommodation options to choose from to make sure you take home great and ‘comfy’ memories. From hotels to homestays and resorts to bungalows; budget to luxury, pick your style of accommodation and we have it here for you. There are plenty of HPTDC guest houses and hotels that provide a comfortable stay at reasonable prices.

Despite the difficult terrain and varying climatic conditions, Himachal is well connected by roads, railways and air. Reach your favourite destination in Himachal from anyplace.
